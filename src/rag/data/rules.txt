Passport Renewal Requirements:
- Applicants must submit their old passport, national ID card (NID), and two passport-size photos.
- Renewal applications must be submitted within 90 days before passport expiry.
- Renewal fee is 1200 BDT for regular service.

New Passport Requirements:
- New applicants submit birth certificate, NID, and two passport-size photos.
- Applicants under 18 require parental consent and a birth certificate.
- New passport fee is 1500 BDT for regular service.

Lost Passport Procedure:
- Lost passport applicants must submit a police report, NID, and recent photos.
- Lost passport replacement fee is 1800 BDT.
